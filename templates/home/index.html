About me

Hi, I'm Tila Ale, a driven and creative web developer. With a passion for Full-stack Web Development, I have honed my skills and gained valuable experience in the industry.

I am currently studying Bachelor of Information Technology at Informatics College Pokhara, where I have learned the latest techniques and honed my ability to bring ideas to life. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects, from ecommerce website to library management system.

My expertise lies in backend development and frontend development, and I am also skilled in MYSQL, Oracle and UI/UX design. I am always looking for new challenges and opportunities to improve and grow, and I am confident that I can bring value to any team.

When I'm not working, I enjoy reading, gardening and photography. I also love to travel and explore new cultures, and I am always on the lookout for my next adventure.

Take a look at my portfolio to see some of my work, and don't hesitate to get in touch if you think I could be the right fit for your next project.
